💡 Key Considerations Along the Journey

  • Time Management: Maximize training during limited free time.
  • Mental Game: Confidence, calmness, and visualization are key.
  • Financial Planning: Secure support or sponsors as you rise.
  • Golf-Specific Fitness: Build the body of a champion.

📆 Summary Timeline

  • Years 1–2: Lower handicap to 18, learn fundamentals, local comps.
  • Years 2–4: Lower to 4, national amateur events, prep for pro.
  • Years 4–6: Play mini tours, qualify for pro circuit.
  • Years 6–10: Build pro career, qualify & win The Masters.
