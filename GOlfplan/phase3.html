Target Goal

Start targeting bigger, higher-level amateur events and work on refining your skills.

1. Compete in National & International Amateur Events

Action: Start targeting prestigious amateur tournaments like the Swedish Amateur Championship and European Amateur Championship.

Once you’re consistently in the low teens or better with your handicap (around 12 or better), you’ll qualify for events with top amateur players.

Travel and experience playing on different courses and conditions to gain exposure.

2. Work on Your Physical Conditioning

Action: Start focusing on golf-specific fitness (strength, mobility, flexibility).

Work on core strength and balance to improve consistency and stamina.

Consider hiring a personal trainer who specializes in golf fitness to improve your game and prevent injuries.

3. Focus on Consistency

Action: Continue practicing short game and putting.

Prioritize course management and mental toughness as you continue to play in higher-level tournaments.

Target Goal: Aim to get your handicap to 4 or better by the end of year 3.
