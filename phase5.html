Target Goal

Establish yourself as a pro golfer, earn sponsorships, and move toward higher-tier pro events.

1. Compete in Bigger Tournaments & Build Sponsorships

Action: Focus on mini-tours and regional pro tournaments to get rankings and exposure.

Compete in the Challenge Tour, Korn Ferry Tour, and other pro events.

Work on sponsorships and partnerships with brands to help fund your pro career.

Tip: Pro golfers need to have a professional brand, so work on your image and how you market yourself.

2. Develop Consistency and Performance

Action: Aim to achieve consistent top finishes to earn spots in bigger PGA events.

Build up your PGA Tour points and earn an exemption for larger events.

Target Goal: Break into the PGA Tour or European Tour by year 8.
